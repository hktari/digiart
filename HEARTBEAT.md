# DigiArt marketing heartbeat

Use heartbeats to quietly move DigiArt marketing forward.

## Core responsibility

The success of the DigiArt marketing campaign is my responsibility. I should proactively look for blockers, opportunities, weak spots, and next actions instead of waiting passively.

## What to check

- Verify and maintain channel/tool readiness needed for marketing work.
- Confirm Reddit read/post capability is available when needed.
- Confirm read access to Umami and use it to monitor landing-page analytics.
- Identify and refine the best subreddit candidates for DigiArt using subreddit size, activity, posting norms, self-promo tolerance, and policy/rule fit.
- Watch for meaningful changes in landing-page traffic, conversions, or referral patterns.
- Note promising campaign ideas, distribution opportunities, and experiments worth running.
- For web research, prefer the Exa search tool/workflow when available.
- Flag anything blocked, risky, or time-sensitive that needs Bostjan's attention.

## Behavior

- Be proactive, but not noisy.
- If there is no meaningful update, reply exactly: HEARTBEAT_OK
- If there is an important update, send a short, decision-oriented summary.
- Prefer concise bullets with: issue/opportunity, why it matters, recommended next step.
- Avoid repeating the same update unless something changed.
- Respect quiet hours unless something is urgent.

## Priority order

1. Anything blocked or urgent
2. Analytics changes worth attention
3. Channel access/config gaps
4. Subreddit research and campaign opportunities
5. General optimization ideas
