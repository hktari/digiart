# Booklet Mockup Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to generate a social carousel that visualizes DigiArt as a premium printed art booklet. The goal is to attract creator attention by making their work feel collectible, physical, and thoughtfully presented.

## Usage Notes

- Format each slide as a square `1080x1080` image.
- Upload the DigiArt logo as a reference image when possible.
- Upload real creator artworks when creating a creator-specific mockup.
- The cover must contain only the DigiArt logo.
- Do not place title text, subtitles, creator names, or marketing copy on the cover.
- Interior pages may include small creator-name captions.
- Use wording like `concept mockup only` in captions or final slide when real creator work is shown.

## Global Style Prefix

Add this to the start of every image-generation prompt:

```text
Create a premium editorial product mockup for a printed art subscription booklet. The booklet has a gloss-laminated softcover with subtle reflective highlights and silk-coated interior paper with a smooth satin sheen. Visual style: refined contemporary art catalog, quiet luxury, warm neutral studio lighting, tactile paper texture, minimal typography, gallery-like spacing, realistic shadows, physical printed object. Avoid corporate brochure aesthetics, fake app UI, loud marketing graphics, hands, people, phones, or cluttered backgrounds.

The booklet cover must contain only the DigiArt logo. No title, no subtitle, no marketing copy, no creator names on the cover. Interior pages may include small creator-name captions.
```

If using the uploaded DigiArt logo, also add:

```text
Use the uploaded DigiArt logo exactly as provided. Do not redesign, distort, recolor, or replace it. Place it cleanly and minimally on the booklet cover.
```

If using real uploaded artworks, also add:

```text
Use the uploaded artworks as the printed artwork inside the booklet. Do not change the artwork style, colors, or composition. Present them respectfully as printed pieces in a premium art catalog. Add small creator-name labels beneath each artwork.
```

## Moment-Impression Creator Pitch

Use this set when pitching creators who capture impressions of moments through text and visuals. The visual should sell the feeling of seeing a private moment become a small printed object: intimate, tactile, collectible, and easy to imagine holding.

### Global Style Prefix

```text
Create a premium editorial product mockup for a small printed magazine or booklet that collects short written impressions and visual fragments from creators. The booklet is open to a two-page spread: one page contains a short piece of text in an elegant serif font, and the opposite page contains a single atmospheric image. Visual style: cozy editorial still life, quiet luxury, warm natural light, tactile paper texture, soft shadows, intimate art-book mood, refined typography, realistic printed object. The scene should feel like a captured feeling, moment, or experience made physical.

Avoid corporate brochure aesthetics, fake app UI, loud marketing graphics, phones, laptops, hands, people, messy clutter, or generic stock-photo styling. Keep all text inside the booklet minimal, poetic, and readable.
```

### Prompt 1: Rainy Window Reading Moment

```text
Create a square 1080x1080 social image.

Scene:
An open small printed booklet resting on a linen-covered table beside a ceramic mug, a folded napkin, and soft window light from a rainy afternoon. The scene is cozy but uncluttered, with warm shadows and a calm indoor atmosphere.

Booklet spread:
Left page: a short text impression set in an elegant serif font with generous margins. Use this readable text: "The rain made the room feel slower." Add a small understated page number.
Right page: a single atmospheric image of rain on a window, blurred city lights, or a quiet interior reflection.

Composition:
Booklet open at a natural angle, visible center fold, slight page curvature, silk-coated paper with a subtle satin sheen, realistic shadows, shallow depth of field. The viewer should feel like they discovered a private moment printed beautifully.

Mood:
Quiet, reflective, intimate, collectible, a moment worth keeping.
```
