# Outreach Snippets for Creator Partnerships

A collection of ready‑to‑use messages for reaching out to potential creators across platforms. Replace the placeholders with the appropriate values for each campaign.

---

## Instagram Direct Message

**Opener**
```
Hey {{CREATOR_NAME}}! I love your recent {{ARTWORK_TYPE}} series. We run a booklet‑first subscription platform that lets artists like you publish physical art books on a recurring schedule while we handle printing, shipping, and support. Would you be interested in a quick chat?
```

**Follow‑up (48‑72 h later)**
```
Hi again {{CREATOR_NAME}}, just checking if you saw my note. Our creators see steady monthly revenue without inventory risk. Let me know if you’d like more details.
```

**Final nudge (one week after follow‑up)**
```
{{CREATOR_NAME}}, I don’t want to flood your inbox—just wanted to say the opportunity is still open and we’re ready to launch the next edition on {{DEADLINE_DAY}}. Happy to answer any questions!
```

## Email Sequence (3‑step)

**Email 1 – Introduction**
*Subject:* “Publish your art in a physical booklet – no upfront costs”
```
Hi {{CREATOR_NAME}},

I’m reaching out from {{PLATFORM_NAME}}. We specialize in turning creators’ work into beautifully printed booklets that are shipped to subscribers on a recurring cadence. You keep all rights; we take care of print, fulfillment, and support.

Would you be open to a short call to explore how this could fit your audience?

Best,
{{YOUR_NAME}}
{{YOUR_EMAIL}}
```

**Email 2 – Value & Details**
*Subject:* “How {{CREATOR_NAME}} can earn recurring royalties”
```
Hey {{CREATOR_NAME}},

Following up on my previous note—here’s a quick snapshot of the model:

- **Royalty split:** {{ROYALTY_SPLIT}}
- **Payout schedule:** {{PAYOUT_SCHEDULE}}
- **Print quality:** Premium matte, 300 dpi (see {{PRINT_QUALITY_NOTES}})
- **Shipping regions:** {{SHIPPING_REGIONS}}

All logistics are handled by us, so you can focus on creating.

Let me know if you’d like to see a sample booklet.

Thanks,
{{YOUR_NAME}}
```

**Email 3 – Closing**
*Subject:* “Last chance to join the upcoming launch on {{DEADLINE_DAY}}”
```
Hi {{CREATOR_NAME}},

Our next production window closes on {{DEADLINE_DAY}}. If you’d like to be part of this edition, we can lock in your spot and start the onboarding process.

Looking forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
{{YOUR_NAME}}
```
