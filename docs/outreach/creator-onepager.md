# Creator One‑Pager

**A concise overview for creators partnering on our booklet‑first art subscription.**

We produce, ship, and support physical art booklets on a recurring cadence, giving creators full control over content while we handle logistics.

---

## Why this works

- **Predictable revenue**: Recurring orders create a stable cash flow for creators.
- **Low upfront risk**: No inventory or upfront printing costs for the creator.
- **Creator‑first cadence**: New editions launch on a fixed schedule, keeping audiences engaged.
- **Full creative control**: Creators decide theme, layout, and timing; we simply execute.
- **End‑to‑end fulfillment**: We manage printing, shipping, and customer support.
