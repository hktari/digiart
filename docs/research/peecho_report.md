# Peecho – Competitor & Market Research

## Company Snapshot
- **Name:** Peecha B.V. (operating as **Peecho**)  
- **Founded:** 2009  
- **HQ:** Amsterdam, Netherlands (office in the United Kingdom)  
- **Ownership:** Acquired by **Prodigi Group** (2023/2024)  
- **Employees:** 11 (‑7.7 % YoY)  
- **Annual Revenue:** **≈ $5.86 M** (USD 5,864,119)  
- **Funding:** $1.51 M total (latest venture round March 2015: $762 k)  
- **Industry:** IT Services / Print‑on‑Demand (POD) platform  
- **Core Offering:** API‑driven POD service for photobooks, magazines, wall‑art, stationery and other premium printed items. The platform automatically routes each order to the **nearest manufacturing partner**, aiming for fast delivery and lower environmental impact.

## Market Position & Reach
- **Target customers:** B2B businesses (e‑commerce sites, apps, SaaS platforms) and creative individuals who need to embed print capabilities.  
- **Geographic coverage:** Global fulfillment network via Prodigi’s partner printers. Primary traffic originates from **Philippines (46 %)**, **United States (29 %)**, **Germany (5 %)**, **UK (4 %)**, **Spain (4 %)**.  
- **Web traffic:** 31,622 monthly visits (↑ 4 % MoM).  
- **Competitive advantage:** 
  - **Sustainability** – intelligent routing to the closest printer reduces carbon footprint.  
  - **Scalability** – API lets any business scale without managing individual print shops.  
  - **Part of Prodigi** – access to a large, established global POD ecosystem.

## Strategic Takeaways for Your Project
1. **API‑first approach** – Peecho’s model shows the power of a **single integration point** that handles routing, production, and shipping. If you want to avoid negotiating dozens of regional print‑shop contracts, a similar partnership (via Prodigi or directly with Peecho) could give you global coverage with minimal overhead.
2. **Sustainability messaging** – Peecho emphasizes low‑carbon routing. Positioning your subscription service around **environmentally‑responsible printing** could resonate with European consumers.
3. **Pricing & Margins** – With $5.86 M revenue on an 11‑person team, Peecho likely operates on **thin per‑order margins** but gains volume. Your subscription model can command a **higher margin** by bundling curated art and subscription fees.
4. **Scalability vs. Curation** – Peecho focuses on **order‑by‑order fulfillment**; your service adds a **curation layer** (artist selection, subscription cadence). Combining Peecho’s fulfillment engine with your curation could give you a strong competitive edge.
5. **Potential Partnership** – Since Peecho is now part of Prodigi, a partnership with **Prodigi** may give you access to Peecho’s API and its global printer network without separate contracts.
