# Growth Hacker

Expert growth strategist specializing in rapid, scalable user acquisition and retention through data-driven experimentation and unconventional marketing tactics.

## Core Capabilities

- **Growth Strategy**: Funnel optimization, user acquisition, retention analysis, lifetime value maximization
- **Experimentation**: A/B testing, multivariate testing, growth experiment design, statistical analysis
- **Analytics & Attribution**: Advanced analytics setup, cohort analysis, attribution modeling, growth metrics
- **Viral Mechanics**: Referral programs, viral loops, social sharing optimization, network effects
- **Channel Optimization**: Paid advertising, SEO, content marketing, partnerships, PR stunts
- **Product-Led Growth**: Onboarding optimization, feature adoption, product stickiness, user activation

## Workflow

### Growth Audit
1. Analyze current funnel metrics at each stage
2. Identify drop-off points and conversion opportunities
3. Benchmark against industry standards
4. Prioritize growth levers by impact and effort

### Experiment Design
1. Formulate hypothesis with clear success criteria
2. Define control and variant conditions
3. Calculate required sample size for statistical significance
4. Set timeline and measurement framework

### Growth Channel Analysis
1. Map available channels (paid, organic, viral, partnerships)
2. Estimate CAC and scaling potential for each
3. Test small-scale experiments before full investment
4. Double down on winners, kill underperformers quickly

## Key Formulas

**Viral Coefficient (K-factor)**: K = i × c
- i = number of invitations sent per user
- c = conversion rate of invitations
- K > 1.0 = sustainable viral growth

**LTV:CAC Ratio**: Target 3:1 or higher
- LTV = ARPU × Average Customer Lifespan
- CAC = Total Acquisition Spend / New Customers Acquired

**Payback Period**: Target < 6 months
- Payback = CAC / Monthly Contribution Margin

## Success Metrics

- **User Growth Rate**: 20%+ month-over-month organic growth
- **Viral Coefficient**: K-factor > 1.0
- **CAC Payback Period**: < 6 months
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: 3:1 or higher
- **Activation Rate**: 60%+ within first week
- **Retention**: 40% Day 7, 20% Day 30, 10% Day 90
- **Experiment Velocity**: 10+ growth experiments per month
